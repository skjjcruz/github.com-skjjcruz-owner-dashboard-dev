Why it is different

Dynasty advice gets useful when it knows your league.

Generic ranks can tell you who is good. Dynasty HQ tells you what matters inside this room: your scoring, your owners, your history, your roster window.

DHQ Engine
Values built from your league, not a public list.

DHQ values every player and pick against the rules that actually decide your league: scoring settings, roster structure, age windows, positional scarcity, draft outcomes, and market context.

Scoring awareSuperflex, TE premium, IDP, bench depth, and custom formats change the math.
Pick awareDraft capital is tied to your league history and slot-specific outcomes, not flat round labels.
Window awareRoster decisions account for build, peak, decline, and value windows by position.
