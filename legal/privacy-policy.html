2. How the App stores data

Some data is stored on your device (for offline use and quick loading). Account and synced data are stored in our cloud database, protected so that you can only access your own records.

3. Third parties we share data with

We do not sell your personal information. We share limited data with service providers strictly to operate the App:

4. Your choices and rights

  • Delete your account and data from within the App at any time (Settings → Delete Account).
  • Access or correct your information by contacting us.
  • Disconnect a linked fantasy platform at any time.
  • Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights (e.g. GDPR/CCPA); contact us to exercise them.

5. Children

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your country), and we do not knowingly collect their data.

6. Data retention

We keep your information while your account is active. When you delete your account, we remove or anonymize your personal data, except where we must retain limited records (e.g. for tax/billing) as required by law.

7. Changes

We may update this policy; we'll revise the effective date and, for material changes, notify you in the App.
